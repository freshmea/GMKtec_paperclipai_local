# Company Research Criteria

This document defines the criteria for evaluating potential robotics hardware and educational software companies.

## 1. Market & Stability
- **Market Position**: Leading presence in the educational robotics sector.
- **Stability**: Proven track record and financial stability of the company.
- **Growth Potential**: Ability to innovate and expand product lines.

## 2. Ecosystem & Support
- **Support Ecosystem**: Availability of technical support, documentation, and community forums.
- **Educational Integration**: Capability of products to integrate into existing school curricula or learning management systems.
- **Software/Hardware Synergy**: Seamless integration between hardware kits and software tools.

## 3. Innovation & Quality
- **Product Quality**: Build quality, reliability, and performance of hardware.
- **R&D Focus**: Commitment to continuous improvement and new educational technologies.
- **Open Source Contribution**: Level of engagement with the open-source community.

## 4. Scalability & Logistics
- **Scalability**: Ability to provide kits for large-scale classroom deployments.
- **Global Availability**: Ease of shipping and availability of localized support/documentation.
