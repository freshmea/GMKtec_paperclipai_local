# Popular Robotics Kits by Age Group

## Early Childhood (Ages 5-8)
- **LEGO Education Spike Essential**: Uses colorful bricks and simple motors to teach basic concepts through play.
- **Bee-Bot**: A simple, programmable floor robot designed for early learners to understand sequencing.

## Middle Childhood (Ages 9-12)
- **LEGO Spike Prime**: A step up from Essential, focusing on more complex builds and sensor integration.
- **VEX IQ**: Uses modular parts to teach engineering principles and basic programming.
- **mBot (Makeblock)**: An entry-level robot kit that is easy to assemble and uses Scratch-based programming.

## Adolescence (Ages 13+)
- **VEX V5**: A professional-grade robotics system used in competitive environments.
- **Arduino**: An open-source electronics platform that allows for deep customization and hardware-level programming.
- **Raspberry Pi**: A powerful single-board computer used for more advanced robotics projects involving AI and computer vision.
- **LEGO Mindstorms (EV3/Spike Prime)**: Continues to be a staple for advanced middle and high school projects.
