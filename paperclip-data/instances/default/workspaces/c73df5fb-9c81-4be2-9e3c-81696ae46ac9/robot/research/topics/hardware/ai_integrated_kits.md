# Research: Advanced Robotics Kits & AI-Integrated Hardware

## 1. AI-Ready & Edge Computing Kits

**Overview**: These kits are designed to run complex AI models (Computer Vision, NLP, SLAM) directly on the robot using edge computing hardware.

### Key Platforms
- **NVIDIA Jetson Series (Nano, Orin)**:
    - **Capabilities**: High-performance GPU-accelerated computing. Can run deep learning models (TensorFlow, PyTorch) in real-time.
    - **Educational Value**: Bridges the gap between standard robotics and AI/Machine Learning.
    - **Use Case**: Autonomous navigation, object recognition, and complex manipulation.
- **Google Coral (Edge TPU)**:
    - **Capabilities**: Optimized for running lightweight TensorFlow Lite models with very low latency and power consumption.
    - **Educational Value**: Teaches efficient AI deployment on resource-constrained hardware.
- **Raspberry Pi (with AI Accelerators)**:
    - **Capabilities**: Versatile single-board computer. When paired with accelerators like the Hailo-8 or Coral, it becomes a powerful AI edge device.
    - **Educational Value**: High versatility for general-purpose computing and robotics integration.

## 2. Specialized Educational Kits

### Maker & Hobbyist Focus
- **DJI RoboMaster**:
    - **Features**: High-end mechanical design, integrated AI capabilities (vision/voice), and a robust SDK.
    - **Best For**: High school and university competitions focusing on advanced autonomy and combat robotics.
- **TurtleBot (ROS-based)**:
    - **Features**: The gold standard for learning ROS. Often based on Raspberry Pi or Jetson.
    - **Best For**: University-level research and advanced robotics development.
