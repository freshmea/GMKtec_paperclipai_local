# Educational Robotics Curricula

## Competitive Robotics Programs
*Highly structured programs designed to foster teamwork, engineering, and problem-solving through competition.*

- **FIRST Robotics (FIRST Tech Challenge, FIRST Robotics Competition)**: A globally recognized program that emphasizes teamwork, engineering, and problem-solving through intense competitions. It covers a wide range of age groups and focuses on real-world engineering challenges.
- **VEX Robotics Competition**: A highly structured program that provides kits and curricula for various age levels, focusing on engineering design and programming. It is widely used in schools and competitive leagues worldwide.

## Structured Educational Curricula
*Designed for classroom integration, providing lesson plans aligned with STEM standards.*

- **VEX Curriculum**: Provides comprehensive lesson plans and activities aligned with educational standards to integrate robotics into the classroom. It is highly effective for K-12 education.
- **LEGO Education Curriculum**: Offers age-appropriate lesson plans and hands-on activities designed to teach STEM concepts using LEGO kits. It is a staple in early childhood and primary education.
- **Arduino Education**: Provides resources and projects to help students learn electronics and programming through real-world applications. Focuses on building foundational skills in embedded systems.

## Online and Self-Paced Resources
*Accessible learning platforms for learners at various stages.*

- **Code.org**: Offers introductory coding and robotics-related lessons for young learners, focusing on foundational computational thinking.
- **Coursera/edX**: Provides more advanced, university-level courses on robotics engineering and programming, suitable for high school students and adult learners.

## Summary of Curriculum Types

| Type | Focus | Target Audience | Key Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Competitive** | Engineering & Teamwork | Middle/High School | High engagement & motivation |
| **Classroom/Structured** | STEM Standards | K-12 Students | Easy teacher integration |
| **Self-Paced** | Individual Learning | All Ages | Flexibility & Accessibility |
