# Research: Sources for Robotics Educational Materials

## 1. Primary Research Sources

### Robotics & AI Trends
- **IEEE Robotics & Automation Society (RAS)**: Leading source for technical advancements and emerging trends in robotics.
- **NVIDIA Developer Blogs**: Insights into edge AI and the integration of deep learning in robotics.
- **ROS.org Documentation**: The authoritative source for ROS/ROS 2 architecture and middleware standards.

### Educational Curricula & Kits
- **FIRST Robotics Official Site**: Information on competitive robotics frameworks and standards.
- **VEX Robotics & LEGO Education**: Product documentation and pedagogical approaches for structured curricula.
- **Arduino & Raspberry Pi Foundations**: Resources for embedded systems and general-purpose computing in robotics.

### Simulation & Software
- **Gazebo & Webots Official Documentation**: Technical details on physics engines and simulation environments.
- **OpenCV Documentation**: Core resources for computer vision implementation.
